ABOUT BERMANO


Let's begin by discussing how I had first discovered the world of NFTs.

I was in New York City, New York when I heard my text chime ring. My phone's screen lit up, showing a text from a close friend's son, Jesse. They were asking if I knew about NFTs; and, at the time I had only heard of the name. Jesse proceeded to make contact with 0xChampi, marking the beginning of our NFT enterprise in which 0xChampi, Jesse and I have dedicated our love, effort and passion to.

Although being diagnosed with Bi-Polar and Depression, I have still managed to focus on creating a thriving art business, painting on canvas. In 2010, I had painted my first painting at the age of 40 and never looked back. I made it my mission through the success of my art works to help the less fortunate and regularly donate to many wonderful causes.

The highlights of my art career include being chosen to paint for Pope Francis, Nelson Mandela and the Royal Family of Monaco.

I am a true minimalist, I don't require much by way of material goods and rather choose to chase the holy grail of inner peace; to work out some of life's biggest questions.

I paint with incredible passion and express myself mainly through color. Each piece crates from my soul. My art represents the joy, love and color that I continuously strive for.

Humbly yours,
Joel Berman aka Bermano
