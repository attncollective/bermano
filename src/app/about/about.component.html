About

How and where do I begin the story of how I became an artist? I suppose at the beginning... Starting at the age of 7. I had severe Obsessive Compulsive Disorder as a young child - OCD is a horrible mental and behavioral disorder where for me it manifested in having to keep on doing the same things over and over and over for reasons which I didn't quite understand. For example, switching a light on and off, or stepping back and forth over a line on the ground countless times. As I grew up, the OCD developed into OCD of thoughts - I began analyzing life at the age of 15. The analysis was kickstarted by the study of the brain in a biology class and it has consumed most of my life, literally spending 95 percent of my waking hours trying to figure out who I am and what I’m doing here. It’s a very tiresome process and I have developed a bad case of bipolar disorder due to these experiences. In a nutshell, I have had a pretty dark cloud hanging over me for a long time. In 2010 I found some spectacular light. It came in the form of answers to my never-ending analysis of what we are doing here. The light shone so brightly that I literally had the overwhelming urge to paint something, and at the time, I was 39 years old and had never painted in my life up to that moment. I went to the art store and bought every color of paint, I went home and painted a Rooster. I became addicted, few months later I gave up my job, and became a full- time artist. It was a very powerful calling - one that I could not resist. It’s like I was sucked into a Canvas. The dark cloud lifted and what came out - should I say *exploded out* as a volcano of color that lay dormant deep inside my soul for so long.
