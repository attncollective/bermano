Who is Bermano/TheRealBermano?
Bermano is a legendary artist born April 9th, 1971 in South Africa. He currently resides in New York City, New York, where he has designed countless beautiful projects.
Throughout his life, Bermano has sold his extravagant artwork to many celebrities, artists, royalty and influential personalities. Some of which include: Pope Francis, Nelson Mandela, the Royal Family of Monaco, dozens of fortune 500 businesses and hundreds of others.
For more information about Bermano and his life, visit our About page or the #about-bermano channel in our official Bermano Digital Gallery Discord server.
